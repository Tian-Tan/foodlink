Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie is a classic dessert made with a flaky pastry crust filled with sliced apples, sugar, and spices, typically cinnamon, and baked until golden and delicious.

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg , + 1 Tbsp water, for egg wash
